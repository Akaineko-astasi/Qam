Intro

What's Going On

This site contains a description for a custom Ragnarök Online job: Qam, the third job for Soul Linker.

Why developing the custom one

These ideas were resting quietly until Gravity has released their work-in-progress: third jobs for the Taekwon branch (link 1, link 2), with Soul Reapers being third job Soul Linkers. Their skills include curses, energy drain and... blowing up a Soul Link and dealing dark property damage.

So the need to show completely different Soul Linkers has emerged.

Shamans, that connect their life with their land and their people.

Ritualists, keeping their laws of conscience.

Those who live both with spirits and humans side by side, and teach their children to be good neighbours and respect the life in any form.

Those who do not betray their promise: Use your powers wisely...

KA-type

KA-skills are blessings or contracts made with spirits to serve the Soul Linker at some special purpose. These include mystical avoidance of any next harm, health recovery on being attacked and even instant resurrection upon death.

KA-type buffs can only be cast on Soul Linker himself, other Soul Linkers and his family members.

The only way to cast KA- on others is to be in the Spirit state (that means, to be buffed with Soul Linker Spirit buff).


KA`STEH

Qam enters the state of guardian spirit, leaving his life behind and following his friend.

Type: KA Switch
Requirements: Crusader Spirit 1, Priest Spirit 1
Casting Time: 1.5 sec
Cooldown: 30 sec
Resources: ~75sp
Effect:

  • your property becomes Ghost 1
  • you cannot use any other skills
  • you have 1 hp and any hit damages your sp instead
  • your hp can not be changed
  • when your defensive target takes physical damage, you take half of it instead (to sp)
  • when your sp reaches 0, the state ends (resulting in 1 hp and 0 sp, coma)
  • when you are more than 14 cells away from your defensive target, the state ends
  • when you are not on the same map, the state ends

ON-type

ON-type magic might be called "Bonfires" because of its environmental features. It often affects the space around the caster, targeting allies, ground or monsters.

Its effects vary from mass blessings to mass status effects, and is cast with open hands: wide, circular gesture.

ON-skills reduce monsters' aggro towards Qam.

TSU-type

TSU-skills may be called Bonds.

TSU effects connect Qam with areas or people and cannot be changed. Vow is a TSU-skill.

Casting of these can be interrupted by Qam's death only.


Soulbond

I offer you my true name.

Type: Active
Target: Player
Duration: once set, cannot be changed (similar to Star Gladiator's Place / Anger choises)
Casting Time: 7 sec (not aff. by Dex)
Resources: 100 sp
Effect:

  • Adds a player to Qam's family. This validates the player for Qam's KA- magic.
  • There will be also a corresponding passive TSU'OH, that adds (0,0,1,1,2) slots.


Shelter

Type: Active
Target: Self
Duration: once set, cannot be changed
Casting Time: 4 sec (not aff. by Dex)
Resources: 100 sp
Effect:Saves your location in the current spot.

  • Available only once.
  • Some MVP maps are available (Audumla), while some dungeons (Lhz. lab) are not.


Cognition

I know your kind.

Type: Active
Target: Monster
Duration: once set, cannot be changed
Casting Time: 4 sec
Resources: 100 sp
Effect: Qam compiles a significant knowledge about the target's race nature and its specific world.
The skill actually works for groups of races. These are:

  • Demihuman (incl. players)
  • Hunter's Four (Brute, Fish, Plant and Insect)
  • Aliens (Demon and Angel)
  • Undead
MATK and ATK are now added together when facing enemies of the chosen race(s).

Features

Prof bonuses

One of the highest Int bonuses (+13) and Dex +12 since Qam has to be a perfect diplomat and a mystic, relying much on the power of his mind as well as his wisdom and perception. Vit +7 since he inherits from Soul Linker dealing with spirits and their cold energies from the Other Side, but also has a strong connection with the wild. Str +3 thanks to survival skills, and others +0. (35 total)

Weapon Proficiency

Qam can wield Daggers and one- and two-handed Staves.
